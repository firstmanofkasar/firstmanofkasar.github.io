About Us


Ram Singh Mehra Homestay is the oldest property in Kasar Devi. It was the first homestay that was started in Kasar Devi in 1974. The family also carries a legacy of setting up the first PCO and Chai Shop in Kasar Devi. We offer both traditional mudhouses as well as the modern luxury rooms depending on the requirement. Property is surrounded with ample of trees, birds, and greenery. The Great Himalayan Ranges are visible from the property. To make our guests feels more homely, we also provide home-cooked traditional meals. The property is easily accessible by roads and is just a kilometer away from the Kasar Devi temple.

Languages spoken: English, Hindi

We discovered this homestay while browsing for places online. There is a history attached to it (being the first homestay of the area, since 1970s) and it is easy to see why. In the recently expanded new building you get comfortable amenities along with an unbeatable view of the mountains Nanda Devi, Panchuli, Trishul and others. While those are what will bring you here first time, what will make you want to come back is prompt and personal service by Suraj, Sanjay and their staff. They are cordial and welcoming for all guests.
